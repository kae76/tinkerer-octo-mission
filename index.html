Summary

My Drupal strengths are in Site Building with the usual supplementary skills: VM management, command line-y when required (oh-my-zsh is AMAZING), issue queue hawker, Sublime text 2 lover, documentation maniac, scrum master neurosis, light theme tweakery... to list a few.

I like finding elegant solutions to problems; solutions which are repeatable and achieved simply with little effort! Automagically is a state of being ~ Zen is more than a theme…

I’m currently working on a MBP, have worked with Ubuntu, Windows, prefer anything that makes life simple and… is Ubuntu ;-) oh and of course I've jumped on the Raspberry Pi bandwagon.

Recently fallen in love with Vagrant – though I’m still learning the weird ways of the headless VM.

I've had a varied career - mostly oscillating between; Art, Design, Technology, Education.
Learning new things drives me; I'm inspired by communities, social change, long hikes, and … quite like a cup of tea. Aside to doing all of the above for work/play etc I also love photography, Taiji, Yoga, hiking, reading... not all at same time.
